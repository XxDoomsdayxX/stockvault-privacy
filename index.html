1

Data We Collect

StockVault collects only the information you provide directly when creating an account or managing your inventory:

  • Account data: first name, last name, date of birth, email address, username
  • Inventory data: item names, quantities, prices, and dates you enter
  • Notification preferences: low-stock thresholds and date alert settings
  • Device information: collected only when you submit a bug report (device model, OS version, app version)
We do not collect location data, contacts, payment information, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.
2

How We Store Your Data

All account and inventory data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

  • Data is protected by Firebase Security Rules — only you can access your data
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase App Check verifies that requests originate from genuine StockVault builds
  • A local cache is maintained on your device for offline use
3

Third-Party Services

StockVault uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, cloud database, and app integrity verification
  • EmailJS — used only when you submit a bug report to deliver your message to our support team

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics tracking, or any other third-party data collection services.

4

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and can delete everything at any time:

  • Open StockVault → tap the 3-dot menu → Delete Account
  • This permanently deletes your account, all inventory data, and your profile from our servers
  • Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone

You may also contact us directly at support@stockvaultapp.com to request manual data deletion.

5

Permissions

StockVault may request the following device permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send low-stock and expiration alerts. Optional and can be revoked in system settings at any time.
  • INTERNET — required to sync your inventory with Firebase and send bug reports.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to detect connectivity for offline/online sync behavior.
6

Children's Privacy

StockVault is not directed at children under the age of 13. Account registration is open to anyone with a valid email. We do not knowingly collect data from minors or adults.

7

Changes to This Policy
