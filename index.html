Privacy Policy

How StockVault collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: March 9, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how StockVault collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our Android application. By using StockVault, you agree to the practices described in this document. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

01
  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account securely.
  • Display name / username — chosen by you during account registration.
  • Inventory data — item names, quantities, prices, categories, and notes you enter.
  • Device information — basic device data collected automatically by Firebase for security and performance.

How We Use Your Information

02
  • To authenticate you securely and maintain your session.
  • To store and sync your inventory data across devices in real time.
  • To deliver optional low-stock and expiration notifications you configure.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access via Firebase App Check.

Data Storage & Security

03

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Each user's data is fully isolated and accessible only by the authenticated account owner. We use Firebase App Check with Play Integrity to ensure only legitimate instances of StockVault can access our backend. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your inventory data is private to your account. No one else — including us — can view it.

Data Sharing

04

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your inventory data is entirely private to your account. Firebase services are provided by Google LLC and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention & Deletion

05

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app via Settings → Delete Account. This action immediately and irreversibly removes all your data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

06

StockVault is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

07

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of StockVault after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, we're here to help.
